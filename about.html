Welcome to Deoda Farm House - A Vision Realized

At Dead Farm House, our story is woven with the dreams and aspirations of the late Shree Mahavavir Chand Deoda Ji. What began as a personal haven has now blossomed into a cherished destination - a luxury resort that caters to the heart's desires for exquisite celebrations, family gatherings, and corporate events.

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    The roots of Dead Farm House trace back to a vision held by Shree Mahavavir Chand Deoda Ji. Initially conceived as a private escape, the farm evolved organically, spurred by the overwhelming response from the Marathwada public. The charm of the place resonated, leading to the inception of hosting family gettogethers post-weddings, gradually becoming a beloved venue for such joyous occasions.

  • Embracing Public Desires

    Fueled by the love and preferences of the Marathwada community, we expanded our offerings. The warm embrace from our patrons motivated us to enhance amenities and accommodations, transforming Dead Farm House into a luxury resort.

  • Our Oasis of Extravagance

    Dead Farm House stands today as a testament to opulence and sophistication. Our Rajyog Bungalow, a symbol of regality, is complemented by a refreshing swimming pool, 25 elegantly designed villas, and two spacious banquet halls. The resort boasts a thoughtfully crafted children's play area, ample parking space, and a spectrum of outdoor and indoor games.

  • Versatility in Celebrations

    Whether it's a destination wedding, a family reunion, or a corporate gathering, Dead Farm House offers a versatile canvas for your celebrations. Our team takes pride in curating unforgettable experiences for our guests, ensuring every event is a seamless blend of luxury and comfort.
